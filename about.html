Our Company

Blue Ocean Overseas Pvt. Ltd. "BO2", established in 2010 under license number 864/066/067 with ISO certification (ISO 9001:2015), stands as a premier and highly recommended manpower agency in Nepal. Entrusted with providing reliable, effective, and trusted recruitment services, BO2 has been supplying diverse categories of human resources across Malaysia, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Mauritius, Japan, European countries, and other locations authorized by the Government of Nepal.

With a decade of expertise, our commitment to quality and professional service delivery has allowed us to satisfy customers and clients worldwide, earning a reputation for excellence in the field of international recruitment.
